# PowerBI-Superstore-Analysis
Superstore Analysis Dashboard

📊 Project Overview
The Superstore Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, product categories, and regional trends using Superstore sales data.
This dashboard provides key insights into business performance through KPI cards, category-wise revenue analysis, and region-wise profit analysis, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions quickly and effectively.

🎯 Objectives
Monitor overall business performance.
Analyze sales, profit, and quantity metrics.
Identify high-performing product categories.
Compare profitability across different regions.
Enable interactive filtering for detailed analysis.

📊 Business Insights
-->Category Performance
   Furniture generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
   Office Supplies contributed significantly to overall sales.
   Technology maintained strong revenue performance.
-->Regional Performance
   West region achieved the highest profit.
   East region ranked second in profitability.
   Central region generated the lowest profit, indicating potential improvement opportunities.
